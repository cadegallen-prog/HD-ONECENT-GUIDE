Clearance Cadence Quick Guide

Cadence A (Most Common)

  • Week 0: $X.00 (first markdown)
  • Week 4: $X.06 (second markdown)
  • Week 8: $X.03 (final markdown)
  • Week 13: $0.01 (penny)

Total time: ~3 months

Cadence B (Faster)

  • Week 0: $X.00 (first markdown)
  • Week 2: $X.04 (second markdown)
  • Week 5: $X.02 (final markdown)
  • Week 7: $0.01 (penny)

Total time: ~7 weeks

Best Categories to Hunt

  • Hardware: Screws, nails, hooks, brackets, fasteners
  • Electrical: Switches, outlets, wire connectors, junction boxes
  • Lighting: Bulbs, fixtures, lamp parts, switches
  • Plumbing: Fittings, connectors, small valves
  • Paint: Accessories, brushes, tape, drop cloths
  • Seasonal: Holiday leftovers, clearance decor

In-Store Hunting Checklist

  1. Check clearance endcaps near main aisles
  2. Scan clearance tags for price endings (.03, .02, .06, .04)
  3. Note markdown dates on yellow tags (2+ weeks old = promising)
  4. Look overhead on top shelves and racks
  5. Check return carts near customer service
  6. Scan items at self-checkout to verify penny status
  7. Inspect locked cases (may contain clearance items)

Checkout Strategy (Quick Version)

Best Method: Self-Checkout

  • Scan the UPC barcode (not clearance tag)
  • Pay immediately if it rings up $0.01
  • Print receipt and keep it
  • Leave quietly without drawing attention

If Stopped by Staff:

  • Stay calm and polite
  • Show your receipt if payment completed
  • Don't argue - walk away if denied
  • Getting banned hurts you and the community

What NOT to Do

DON'T Why Not
Ask staff for "penny items" They'll pull them immediately
Scan the yellow clearance tag Freezes terminal and alerts staff
Go to the same store daily You'll get recognized and watched
Brag or film inside stores Draws attention and tightens enforcement
Argue with employees Gets you banned permanently

Digital Pre-Hunt (App Strategy)

  1. Open Home Depot app and enable location
  2. Search categories: hardware, lighting, electrical, paint
  3. Filter by "Clearance" or sort by "Price: Low to High"
  4. Look for items under $5 with .03, .02, .06, .04 endings
  5. Check "Store Availability" at target location
  6. Screenshot SKU and barcode for in-store reference
  7. Visit store within 24â€“48 hours (penny status is time-sensitive)
