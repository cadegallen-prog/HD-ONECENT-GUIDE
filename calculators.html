      Should I Buy This? Decision Helper

      Interactive decision tree to help you evaluate whether a penny item is worth purchasing and reselling.

      Question 1: Is there demand for this item?

      Check eBay sold listings, Facebook Marketplace, or other platforms. Are similar items selling?

      Question 2: Is the item in sellable condition?

      New in box is ideal, but minor cosmetic issues are often okay. Major damage limits resale value.

      Question 3: Do you have space to store it?

      Large items tie up storage and may take longer to sell. Consider your available space.

      Question 4: Is the market saturated?

      Are dozens of people already selling the same item? Market saturation can make it hard to sell.

      Question 5: Can you sell it quickly?

      Fast-moving items free up capital. Consider if this will sell within 1-2 months.
