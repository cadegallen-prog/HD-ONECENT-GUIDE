No Guru BS—Just Real Info

This isn't a "secret sale" guide or some exclusive insider hack. Penny items exist because of gaps in Home Depot's inventory removal process—nothing more.

Everything here is based on:

  • Consistent community observations
  • Shared receipts and screenshots
  • Retail clearance logic
  • Trial and error from real shoppers

Important: This guide reflects what the penny shopping community has learned over time. It's not official Home Depot policy, and practices vary by store.

Final Heads-Up

Penny items aren't meant to be sold. They're marked for removal. If you manage to buy one, it's because something slipped through the cracks.

Stores are actively trained to pull these items and stop these sales. Your success depends on staying low-key, respectful, and smart about it.
