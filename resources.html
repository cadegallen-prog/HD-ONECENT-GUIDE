How to Use These Resources

For Printing:

  • Click "View & Print" on any template
  • Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)
  • Select "Save as PDF" to keep a digital copy
  • Print on standard letter-size paper (8.5" x 11")

For Digital Use:

  • Save templates as PDFs to your phone or tablet
  • Take screenshots for quick reference while shopping
  • Bookmark pages you reference frequently

For Customization:

  • Right-click and "View Page Source" to see the HTML
  • Copy and modify templates to fit your needs
  • All resources are open and freely adaptable
