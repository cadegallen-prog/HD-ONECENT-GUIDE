Store Visit Checklist

Print this checklist for an organized, efficient penny hunting trip.

Before You Leave Home

Phone fully charged

You'll need it for scanning barcodes and checking the Home Depot app

Home Depot app open and logged in

Pre-scout clearance items and save time in-store

Payment method ready

Debit/credit card works best at self-checkout

Reusable bag or basket

Carry items discreetly while hunting

Target store location confirmed

Know hours, address, and parking situation

Section-by-Section Hunt

Clearance Endcaps (Main Aisles)

Check all endcaps near main walkways for clearance items

Hardware Department

Screws, nails, hooks, brackets, fasteners - high penny volume

Electrical Department

Switches, outlets, wire connectors, junction boxes

Lighting Department

Bulbs, fixtures, lamp parts, switches

Plumbing Department

Small fittings, connectors, valves

Paint Department

Accessories, brushes, tape, drop cloths

Seasonal Section

Holiday leftovers and off-season decor

Return/Overstock Carts

Check near customer service for clearance items being restocked

Overhead Shelving

Use yellow ladders to access top shelves (stay safe!)

Locked Cases

Scan items through glass or ask for access with a keeper item

Price Tag Inspection

Look for .03 or .02 endings

Final markdown - most likely to go penny soon

Look for .06 or .04 endings

Second markdown - check back in 1-2 weeks

Check markdown dates on tags

Items marked down 2+ weeks ago are most promising

Note multiple units of same SKU

More units = better chance some haven't been pulled

Scanning and Verification

Scan UPC barcodes (not clearance tags)

Use self-checkout or the Home Depot app to verify prices

Screenshot or note SKU numbers

Record promising items for future reference

Check app for "Limited Stock" or "Out of Stock" items

These may still be on the floor but marked for removal

Checkout Process

Go to self-checkout (preferred)

Most discreet method for penny items

Have a "keeper" item ready

$10-20 item you actually need to make transaction look normal

Scan UPC barcode (not clearance tag)

Avoid freezing the terminal

If it rings up $0.01, pay immediately

Don't hesitate or second-guess

Print receipt and keep it

Your proof of purchase

Leave quietly without celebrating

Don't draw attention to your find

If Stopped by Staff

Stay calm and polite

Never argue or cause a scene

Show receipt if transaction completed

Proof that you paid the scanned price

If denied, walk away gracefully

One item isn't worth getting banned

After Your Visit

Log what you found (or didn't)

Use Store Notes Template to track patterns

Note store layout and clearance locations

Make future visits more efficient

Record staff behavior and policies

Some stores are stricter than others

Plan next visit date

Return in 10-14 days to avoid being recognized

Share useful finds with community

Help others by posting SKUs, dates, and details
